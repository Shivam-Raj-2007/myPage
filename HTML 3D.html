The Seven Wonders Of The World


5). Christ the Redeemer:--

Christ the Redeemer (Portuguese: Cristo Redentor, standard Brazilian Portuguese, local pronunciation:
is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, created by French sculptor Paul Landowski
and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa, in collaboration with French engineer Albert Caquot.
Romanian sculptor Gheorghe Leonida fashioned the face. Constructed between 1922 and 1931, the statue is 30
metres (98 ft) high, excluding its 8-metre (26 ft) pedestal. The arms stretch 28 metres (92 ft) wide.
